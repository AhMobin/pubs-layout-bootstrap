Author Guideline for Journal

Language

Research work or all articles should be submitted in English language. Other languages will not be accepted.

Paper Submission

Manuscripts should be submitted by one of the authors via email: (our SheBa Pubs email id).Manuscript should be Microsoft word (.doc, .docx) files. There is no page limit in our journals. Submission authors will get paper ID number and ID number can use for future corresponding reference purpose. Papers must be submitted original work. The submitted Author is responsible for ensuring that the articles have been approved by the other co-authors and affiliation.

Review Process

Submitted article will send to review process and review will send for authors if needed any modification or change.

Publication Fee

SheBa Pubs is an open publishing service and publication charge are need for article maintenance, long times keeps for archives. All reader cans read article absolutely free and readers can print, download and copy. (Before publication author needs to pay publication fee and after made payment author needs to send payment made receipt to publisher.)

Units Measurement

System International (SI) units.

Paper Title

The following information should be included in all submitted article:

  • Paper title [Times new roman and 20 Bold]
  • Full author names [Times new roman and 12 Bold]
  • Full affiliation mailing addresses
  • Phone and fax number
  • Email addresses
Abstract [Times new roman and 12 Bold]

The manuscript abstract word should be 200-300 words. The abstract should include background, experiment, and conclusion. [Text 10 point with times new roman]

Introduction [Times new Roman and 12 Bold]

This section should be brief discussion and 400-600 words [Text 10 point with times new roman]

Materials and Method [Times new Roman and 12 Bold]

This section needs to describe the research materials and method. [Text 10 point with times new roman]

Results and Discussion [Times new Roman and 12 Bold]

This section needs to describe experimental results and analysis result. [Text 10 point with times new roman]

Conclusion [Times new Roman and 12 Bold]

This section needs to include overall full original work. [Text 10 point with times new roman]

Acknowledgements [Times new Roman and 12 Bold]

Acknowledgements should be included at the end of the paper and before the references. It may also include supporting grants, private money, etc. [Text 10 point with times new roman]

References [Times new Roman and 12 Bold]

References section need to write down like Authors name, paper title, journal name, vol., no., year, page no., references should be cited within the text in number wise. [Reference text 10 points with times new roman]

Figure and Table's

Figure and Tables should be cited serially in the text area with number. Table should be three line tables. No vertical line in the table.
Figure format: Photoshop, TIFF, GIF, JPEG.

Typeset Proof

Accepted paper will send to authors and authors need to resend publisher within 2 - 4 days for final publication.

Copyright Form

Authors need to sign the copyright form before final publication and sent to the publisher that the original work.

Author Guideline for Book

To publish your manuscript quickly and to a high standard, we request all authors to follow these guidelines when preparing their manuscript.

Who to connect with:

Within the Editorial Services Unit, the Administrative Editors are the main point of contact during the publication process that deals with questions concerning both editorial and production issues. Your initial contact with SheBa Publication will be through the respective Publishing Editor for your subject area.

What we're looking for from you:

An electronic and a hard copy of the manuscript will have to be provided by the Author. The hard copy must be identical to the electronic copy. Before you save it on your disk, please ensure that you print the hard copy from the computer file.

  • Microsoft Word for Windows is strongly preferred for electronic manuscripts.
  • For a paper copy, the manuscript will be printed on one side of the A4 (US or European).
Guideline for manuscript preparation:

Your manuscript should include the followings:

  • Times Roman for all text and tables is preferable. By using this font throughout the manuscript will reduce problems at typesetting, especially with tables.
  • Typing instructions:
    • Distinguish the letter 1 from the digit 1
    • For indents tabs are preferable, not space
    • Headings and titles should be displayed consistently
    • Double space between all manuscript lines is preferable; single spacing is only acceptable on tables.
  • References: References should follow the standard of the APA (American Psychological Association). References should be indicated by giving (in parentheses) the Name of the Author followed by the date of the paper or book; or the date in parentheses, as suggested by Fletching (1975).
    For Books: Zeleny, M. (1982). Multiple Criteria Decision Making. New York: McGraw-Hill.
  • Tables and figures: Each table or figure should be started on a separate page. In the text, indicate the approximate position of each table with a clear text break. All figures and tables should be numbered in sequence ( e.g., table 1, figure 1).
  • Graphics and illustrations: These should be sent on a separate diskette or other media format. For all artwork, graphs and figures Camera Ready Copy should be provided as well as submitting artwork electronically.
  • Permission to reprint: If you use material from a copyrighted work (e.g., tables, figures or an extract from a book that exceeds 400 words), you will need written permission from the copyright holder (mostly the publisher) to use this material. It is the responsibility of the Author to get permission for the reprint. A copy of the manuscript must accompany permission.

  • Label your disk clearly: You have to label the disk with your Name, the file name, the Name of the book to be published in and the Name of the software used (e.g. MS Word 97 for Windows, MS Word 5 for Mac). If scanned graphic files are included, please state the resolution used.

Track your Article
Track your submitted paper

You can track your research status that submitted. The tracking system is same both for submissions and submitted article. Use the reference number you received after submission to track your submission.

Track your accepted paper

You will receive a reference number and a direct link when your paper is accepted for publication that lets you follow its publication status via SheBa Pub's 'Track Your Accepted Article' service.

And, Even without having a notification you can track your article status by entering your article reference number and corresponding author surname in Track Your Accepted Article.

How can I track my accepted article?

For tracking your accepted article's progression in a easiest and quickest way, you can use SheBa Pub‟s Article Tracking service. You can also view bibliographic information (once available), link to the published version of your article on Science Direct, and view the Scopus citation count for your article.

We recommend registering an account with the tracking service. Registering is a free and simple process and enables you to:

  • Create your own personalized home page ('My Tracked Articles') with links to the detailed tracking information for all your SheBa Pub's.
  • Save your article's details – just log into your account and the list of articles you've previously added for tracking will be available.
How
