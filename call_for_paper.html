Submission Open for SheBa Publication - 2020

SheBa Publication providing a platform for the researchers, academicians, professionals, practitioners and students to impart and share knowledge in the form of high quality empirical and theoretical research papers, case studies, literature reviews, International Journal of Education and Research welcomes and acknowledges high quality theoretical and empirical original research papers, case studies, review papers, literature reviews, book reviews, conceptual framework, analytical and simulation models, technical note from researchers, academicians, professional, practitioners and students from all over the world.

SheBa Publication publishes both print and online version of journal, magazine and book. SheBa Publications publishes research paper in the field of:

Social science and Humanities: Social Science and Humanities, Sociology, Social Welfare, Anthropology, Religious Studies, Visual Arts, Political, Cultural Aspects of Development, Tourism Management, Public Administration, Psychology, Philosophy, Political Science, History, Education, Women Studies and so on.

Biological Science: Biosciences, Natural science, Biophysics, Neurobiology, Physiology, Marine Biology, Microbiology, Environmental Biology, Forensic Biology, Computational Biology and many others.

Biology: : Zoology, Botany, Biochemistry, Anatomy, Cytology, Ecology, Taxonomy, Genetics, Cell & molecular biology, Bioengineering and so on.

Business & Economics: Business & Marketing, Economics, Financial Development, Accounting, Banking, Management, Human Resources etc.

Chemistry: Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry, Biochemistry, Industrial Chemistry, Polymer Chemistry, Environmental Chemistry and so on.

Climate: Biodiversity & climate change, Climate change effect, Global Warming, Air pollution, Environmental Analysis and many more.

Computer Science: : Software development & testing, Web applications and databases, Algorithms & data structures, Computer science & Robotics etc.

Earth Science: : Geology, Oceanography, Meteorology, Astronomy etc.

Economics: : Fundamental economics, Macroeconomics, Microeconomics, International Economics, Personal finance economics etc.

Energy: : Solar energy, geothermal energy, wind energy, hydrogen energy, tidal energy, hydroelectric energy, wave energy and so on.

Engineering and technology: : Web technology, Paper battery, Communication network design, Information and Communication tech etc.

Environment Science: : Social sciences, Geosciences, Environmental Chemistry, Ecology, Atmospheric Sciences and so on.

Food Science: : Food Microbiology, Food Engineering & Processing, Food Chemistry & Biochemistry, Nutrition, Sensory Analysis etc.

Geography: : Natural resources, Cultural practices, Socioeconomic and political systems, Global positioning Systems (GPS), Remote Sensing (RS) and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) etc.

Life Science: : Agriculture, Fisheries & Food, Anatomy & Morphology, Behavioral Sciences, Biology, Biochemistry & Biotechnology and so on.

Mathematics: Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus, Statistics & Probability, Applied Mathematics, Cartesian Geometry and many others.

Microbiology: Environmental Microbiology, Pathogenicity & Virulence, Biotechnology & Synthetic Biology, Micro biomes, Food Microbiology and so on.

Medicinal Microbiology: Bacteriology, Mycology, Parasitology, Phycology, Pathology etc.

Pharmaceutical Science: Drug discovery & design, Drug delivery, Drug action, Drug analysis, Clinical sciences etc.

Physics: Classical mechanics, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism, Statistical mechanics, Quantum mechanics and many others.

Psychology: Social cognition, attribution theory, social influence, group processes, prejudice and discrimination and so on.

Social Science: Anthropology, Archaeology, Economics, Geography, History, Law, Linguistics, Politics etc.

Statistics: Inferential statistics, Descriptive statistics, Applied Statistics, Mathematical Statistics and so on.

Water Chemistry: Natural water, Water composition, Water quality, Water pollution, Waste water treatment, Environmental water chemistry etc.

Water Chemistry: Natural water, Water composition, Water quality, Water pollution, Waste water treatment, Environmental water chemistry etc.

Acceptance Notification: Within 5-7 days from the date of manuscript submission.

Submission Open: Submission open from October 15, 2020.
